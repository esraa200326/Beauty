Common questions about product

Is the Dermactive Act: Clear Gentle Cleansing Gel suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes! It's specially formulated to be non-drying and soap-free, making it gentle enough for sensitive and combination skin types.

Can I use this cleansing gel twice a day?

Yes, this product is gentle enough to be used twice daily - once in the morning and once in the evening as part of your skincare routine.

Does it help with acne or oily skin?

Absolutely! The formula is designed to effectively cleanse oily and acne-prone skin without disrupting the skin barrier. It helps control excess oil production while being gentle on the skin.

Can I use it with other skincare products?

Yes! This cleansing gel works well with most skincare products. Use it as the first step in your routine, followed by toners, serums, and moisturizers. It provides a clean base for all your other skincare products to work effectively.

How long does one bottle last?

A typical bottle can last 2-3 months with daily twice-a-day use, depending on the amount you use each time. A little goes a long way, so just apply a small amount to damp skin.
